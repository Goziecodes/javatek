IMPROVING THE LIVES OF YOUTHS AND YOUNGSTERS IN THE AREA OF TECHNOLOGY. WE HAVE ALSO AFFECTED REGIONS WITH HIGH LEVEL OF TECH-ILLITERACY AND BROUGHT SOME OF THESE AREAS UP TO SPEED THROUGH VARIOUS FORMS OF EMPOWERMENT.
